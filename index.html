tip 1 - tidy up your cables

Using simple office supplies like a bulldogs clips clamped at the end of your desk, are a great way to clean up on that cable clutter.

tip 2 - use your keyboard to scroll on web pages

Not a lot of people know this, but hitting SPACE when on a webpage will scroll down! SHIFT + SPACE will scroll up, who knew!?

tip 3 - no internet? no problem!
t-rex has got your back!

No internet? Lack of dopamine from the lack of content getting you down? T-Rex has got your back! If you hit SPACE on chrome when you are no connected to the internet will treat you to a little mini-game. Try it out!

tip 4 - Lego mini-figure hands holding
cords for your benifit

Not only can you play with legos, but these little guys make for great cable managment tools! The hands of these guys can accomadate most cables, Not a bad option if your looking to flair up your desk Aesthetic.

tip 5 - find your phone

If you are clumsy like me, then you are bound to have lost your phone at some point. right? If you are on Android and halve lost your phone, then going to android.com/find will bring you to handy little tool to help you find your lost device!

tip 6 - use a gift card for a free trial

Don't tell anybody I told you this, but for most sites when you sign up for the first time you get a free trial! by using a gift card you can essentially do this more than once! most sites will keep a record of your credit card so that you can't use it more than once for a free trial.
